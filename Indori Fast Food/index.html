About Indori Fast Food

Indori Fast Food is a simple online ordering experience inspired by Indore’s iconic street food culture. It’s built to feel like a real ordering app with a clean, fast and mobile-friendly UI.

The layout is designed with modern food apps in mind: clear cards, transparent pricing, quick filters, and a responsive menu grid.
